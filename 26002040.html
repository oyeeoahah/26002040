Understand how scammers operate

  • By deceiving individuals into giving them money or personal information, often through psychological manipulation and exploiting vulnerabilities
  • They use various methods, including impersonation, phishing, fake offers, and social engineering, to gain trust and create a sense of urgency or fear
  • Scammers frequently target victims through phone calls, emails, texts, and social media, and may use fake profiles and fabricated stories to build rapport
